== Description ==

Blogband is an easy to use blogging theme with  minimal and modern design. It is a free theme with a responsive design, over four different blog layouts. Blogband is perfect for your personal blog, magazine , news , business , fashion , fitness , shop , education , health ,  ecommerce or any content-focused website. It is a simple blog and magazine design and multi-purpose theme compatible with elementor , divi , gutenberg page builder and woocommerce.
